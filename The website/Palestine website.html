Steadfastness:
A History Of Endurance

Steadfastness: A History of Endurance is a digital exhibition exploring the cultural and political resilience of the Palestinian people through archival posters, poetry, photographs, and printed ephemera. Centered around the enduring concept of sumud—steadfastness—it examines how Palestinians have responded to displacement, occupation, and fragmentation with acts of memory, expression, and resistance. From the 1948 Nakba to contemporary struggles, these materials document not only the violence of war, but also the persistence of identity and the fight to remain rooted in land, language, and heritage. This is not a static archive, but a living testimony to a people who have refused erasure.
