Narration

Choose your path

This page is your entry point to our collection. You can choose to consult the items based on their historical timeline or through a more storytelling-based narration designed to illuminate the pivotal role of art and archive practices as acts of resistence against genocide.

To support and maximise your experience, we offer two distinct viewing modes that you can comfortably change through the switch button in the top-left part of the screen. You can choose between:

  • Immersive mode: for a truly immersive and emotional theme layout
  • Minimal mode: a clean and minimalist alternative, focused more on item consultation.
We hope you'll enjoy your visit.
