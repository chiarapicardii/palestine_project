Progetto MMMM: Documentazione Tecnica

1. Introduction

This is where you describe the aim and scope of your project.

2. Research & Planning

Summarize your preliminary research, tools used, or cultural questions posed.

3. Design Process (Deliberate Design)

Explain how you chose your structure, layout, and technology stack. (Qui andrà la giustificazione dei temi Immersive e Minimal).

4. Development Steps

Break down your development work step by step (e.g., HTML, CSS, interactivity).

5. Challenges & Solutions

Write about any difficulties faced and how you solved them.

6. Final Reflection

Reflect on what you learned and what could be improved in future iterations.
