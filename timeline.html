Timeline

This timeline collects various items: photographs, posters, documents from the 20th century, specifically extending from 1936 to the 1990s. While these pieces show pivotal moments in Palestinian history, like the 1948 exodus and the British colonization, their purpose goes further: they serve as a historical context to highlight the strength and resilience of the people who, to this very day, continue to fight for their lives and their land.

The intent of this collection is not merely to create a chronological listing of the items. Instead, we aim to paint a picture that diverges from the usual framing, which sees them either as vulnerable victims or as ruthless terrorists ‚Äì failing to capture the complexity and multiple layers that characterize any real community.

This is precisely why we decided to include images of daily life; they provide a crucial counterpoint, reminding us that Palestinians, beyond newspaper headlines and polarized narratives, exist, maintain their traditions, and can reorganize themselves in the face of highly traumatic changes.
